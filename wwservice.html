Estate planning is a systematic approach to distribute one's estate /property according to his wishes in future after his death.

Pass On Your Hard Earned Wealth Easily To Your Family

WHAT IS A WILL?

A "Will" is a document made by a testator (will writer) in the written form stating the manner in which his property must be distributed after his death.
Will document is a very important and easy tool to ensure smooth distribution of wealth to your loved ones as per your wishes.
What are the consequences of dying Intestate (Dying without writing a Will)?
The major disadvantage is, no opportunity to appoint guardian for your minor children. This means that in your absence the court will appoint guardian for your children.
Lots of time and money will be wasted, just to locate and consolidate your assets/property
Distribution of the assets as per the succession laws of the religion, so all the legal heirs will get equal proportion in the property irrespective of wishes of the testator.
Your legal heirs have to obtain Succession certificate for distribution of your Financial assets.

WHAT ARE THE ADVANTAGES OF WRITING A WILL?

Consolidation of All Assets
Appoint Guardian for your Children
Avoid disputes over property
Distribute Assets as per your Wish
Get peace of Mind

WHO CAN WRITE A WILL?

Any person above 18 years of age
Having sound mind
Free from any influences
Not debarred from making a Will
